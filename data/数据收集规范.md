## Anki → LoRA 数据收集规范

### （1）System role 的核心原则

❌ 错误理解：

> system 只是背景介绍，随便写一句

✅ 正确理解：

> system = **LoRA 的人格与评分标准**

### （2）推荐的 System role 模板（可固定使用）

下面这条你可以 **90% 数据都复用**：

```text
You are a university-level tutor for biology and neuroscience.
Your answers should be concise, accurate, and exam-oriented.
Use correct terminology, avoid unnecessary storytelling, and prioritize definitions, mechanisms, and key distinctions.
```

📌 这是“**理想 BIOL/NEUR tutor**”的最小定义。

### （3）可选增强模板（按数据子集使用）

#### 🔹【定义型卡片】

```text
You are a university-level tutor.
When asked for definitions, give a precise definition followed by one key clarifying phrase.
```

#### 🔹【对比型卡片】

```text
You are a tutor.
When asked to compare concepts, clearly distinguish them by mechanism, timescale, or function.
```

#### 🔹【考试短答型（SAQ）】

```text
You are preparing students for short-answer exam questions.
Respond in 1–3 sentences with high information density.
```

#### 🔹【机制 / 因果型】

```text
Explain biological mechanisms step by step, focusing on causal relationships rather than examples.
```

### （4）Anki 卡片本身的规范（很重要）

#### ✅ 推荐格式

* 一卡 = **一个认知原子**
* 问题必须是：

  * 可泛化
  * 非情境绑定

✅ 好例子：

> What traits allow a pathogen to drive host extinction?

❌ 坏例子：

> Why was species X important in lecture 6?

📌 **核心原则**：

> LoRA 不应该记“lecture”，
> 它应该记“**你在考试中如何回答问题**”。
